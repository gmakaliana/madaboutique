What We Offer

Our items from Johannesburg and Durban are fashionable,affordable and available!

Selling items

  • We sell new clothes and take orders of clothes from the age of 9-10 years.
  • We sell new shoes from size 3 to 8.
  • We sell suits.
  • We sell bags.
  • We sell hats.
  • We sell watches.
  • We sell second hand branded shoes from size 3 to 8.
  • We sell furniture.

We take orders

  • We take orders of items like clothes,shoes and furniture with a deposit of 50% and settle the balance when you receive your order.
  • Remember that you have every right to send us the details of items you want, and ours is to look or buy for you.
  • Please note that we buy you these items at their original prices and yours is simply pay us runner fee.

We deliver items

  • We deliver the ordered items around Maseru to our customers.

Where to find us

  • We are located at Maseru Lesotho
  • Opposite Puma filling station
